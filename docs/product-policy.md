# Product policy

souq sells **only legal digital products with clear rights**. We are not
a grey-market store and never present ourselves as one.

## What we sell

- Templates (Notion, Word, Excel, Figma) authored by us or our partners
- Spreadsheets (financial models, planners, calculators)
- Prompt packs (curated, original prompt collections)
- E-books and guides authored by us
- Code snippets, components, boilerplates we wrote or licensed
- Mini-courses delivered as digital files
- Curated bundles of the above

## What we never sell

- Unauthorised resells of paid software, courses, e-books
- Pirated PDFs, cracked apps, leaked databases
- "Universal" license keys, OEM keys, account credentials
- Personal data, scraped contact lists, leaked credentials
- Anything covered by an active DMCA notice

## Listing requirements

Every product must declare:

- A clear license type (`personal_use`, `business_use`, `commercial_use`,
  `resale_rights`)
- Provenance — written by us, licensed from author X, etc.
- Refund eligibility (default: 30 days unless explicitly excluded)
- Content language(s) for the actual files (separate from UI language)

The compliance agent (Sprint 6) will block listings that miss any of the
above.
